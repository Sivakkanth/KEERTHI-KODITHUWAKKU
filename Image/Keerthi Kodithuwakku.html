Professional Background

Keerthi's professional journey commenced within the esteemed halls of the University of Moratuwa, where his insatiable curiosity and passion for design and programming set him apart from his peers. While others adhered to conventional learning, Keerthi embarked on a path of daring projects that would lay the foundation for his future endeavors.

Beyond the confines of academia, Keerthi's influence extended profoundly. Recognising the need to cultivate a spirit of entrepreneurship and leadership among students, he founded the Entrepreneurship Society at the university, an initiative that continues to thrive under his guidance to this day. This endeavor challenged prevailing cultural norms and fostered a shift from socialist to capitalist ideals, transforming the mindset of young minds. Keerthi believed in channeling their raw energy from protests to more constructive endeavors, instilling a belief that one must be the change they wish to see in the world.

Keerthi's early leadership and innovative spirit would become defining traits of his career. His journey in biomedical engineering, IoT technologies, and pioneering software solutions exemplifies his challenges alike, Keerthi continues to shape the future of technology, leaving an enduring impact on the world around him. As his story unfolds, Keerthi's journey serves as a testament to the transformative power of leadership and unwavering dedication, inspiring generations to embrace innovation and drive positive change in their communities and beyond.
